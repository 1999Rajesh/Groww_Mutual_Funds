# ⚠️ Disclaimer - Mutual Funds RAG Assistant

## Important Notice

**This chatbot is for informational and educational purposes only.**

### What This Tool Does
- Provides factual information about HDFC Mutual Fund schemes from official sources
- Answers queries using retrieved data from AMC websites, Groww platform, and regulatory bodies
- Offers quick access to fund details, expense ratios, categories, and general mutual fund information

### What This Tool Does NOT Do
- ❌ **Does NOT provide investment advice or recommendations**
- ❌ **Does NOT predict future performance or returns**
- ❌ **Does NOT suggest which funds to buy or sell**
- ❌ **Does NOT offer personalized financial planning**
- ❌ **Does NOT replace professional financial advisory services**

### User Responsibilities
1. **Verify Information**: Always cross-check critical information with official AMC documents, scheme information documents (SID), and fact sheets
2. **Consult Advisors**: Speak with a SEBI-registered investment advisor before making investment decisions
3. **Read Documents**: Review Scheme Information Document (SID), Key Information Memorandum (KIM), and fact sheets
4. **Understand Risks**: Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks; past performance doesn't guarantee future results

### Data Sources
Information is sourced from:
- Official HDFC AMC website (hdfcfund.com)
- Groww investment platform (groww.in)
- AMFI India (amfiindia.com)
- SEBI (sebi.gov.in)
- CAMS (camsonline.com)

### Accuracy & Limitations
- Information may not be real-time; NAV and portfolio data change daily
- The assistant uses AI and may occasionally misinterpret context
- Confidence scores are provided to indicate reliability
- Users should verify current data on official platforms

### Regulatory Compliance
This tool complies with:
- SEBI guidelines on investor education
- AMFI best practices for mutual fund distribution
- Data privacy and intellectual property rights
